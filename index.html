few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the sun and is the only known planet to support life, characterized by its large amounts of liquid water, a protective atmosphere, and active plate tectonics. It's an oblate spheroid with an approximate diameter of 12,756 km, composed of a solid inner and outer core, a mantle, and a rocky crust. The planet orbits the sun in about 365 days and rotates on its axis, completing one rotation in approximately 24 hours. Its surface is about 71% water and 29% land, with one moon that influences tides and helps stabilize the climate
